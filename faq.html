How does Remee help me realize I am dreaming?

In default mode, Remee waits towards the end of the night when most people are in longer, deeper periods of REM sleep - these are the cycles where you are most likely to experience vivid dreams. Your dream signal will play at specific intervals of your choosing during these later stages of sleep, increasing the chances of catching you while dreaming.
